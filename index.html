What does Housing Matters Cover?

Housing Matters is a framework that provides all necessary functionality to support key housing management processes like:

  • Rentals, Homeownership Opportunities
  • Section 8, Public Housing Vacancies
  • Pre, Post-Assignment Processes

Rentals, Homeownership Opportunities

Setting up Studios, 1, 2,3,4,5-Bedroom units using HUD specifications for income based on unit type.

  • Diverse Housing Options: From cozy studios to spacious 5-bedroom units, our rental and homeownership opportunities cater to individuals
  • HUD Specifications: Our units are designed and set up according to HUD specifications, ensuring compliance with guidelines for income-based housing.
  • Income-Based Housing: By utilizing HUD specifications, we create an inclusive environment where housing is not only available but also affordable for everyone.
  • Tailored Solutions: We understand that each individual or family has unique housing needs and financial circumstances. That's why we offer personalized solutions, including flexible lease options, financial.

Section 8, Public Housing Vacancies

Setting up leased and public housing units within the system, and managing waitlists for these opportunities using Housing Choice Voucher Program.

  • Accessible Housing Opportunities: We facilitate access to Section 8 and public housing vacancies, providing individuals and families with affordable housing options through the Housing Choice Voucher Program.
  • Streamlined Management: Our team efficiently manages waitlists for leased and public housing units within the system, utilizing the Housing Choice Voucher Program to allocate resources effectively.
  • Transparency and Accountability: We operate with transparency and accountability, ensuring that the allocation of leased and public housing units is conducted in a fair and equitable manner.

Pre, Post-Assignment Processes

  • Comprehensive Housing Setup: We oversee the entire process of setting up housing opportunities, from accepting and reviewing applications to assigning units based on eligibility criteria.
  • Efficient Assignment Management: Our team manages post-assignment activities with precision and efficiency, handling tasks such as rent payments, lease renewals, move-outs, and transfers.
  • Tenant Support and Assistance: We prioritize tenant satisfaction by providing ongoing support and assistance throughout the pre and post-assignment processes.

We built Housing Matters based on real world consulting experience in this discipline. We realized that there is now a growing need to make affordable housing accessible. For this to happen, we needed to build a product that addresses all aspects of housing opportunity setup and management, and that connected the dots between the interests of housing authorities, real estate development companies, and the public.

Housing Matters is a suite of software solutions seamlessly integrated to facilitate management of large-scale affordable housing projects at all points of user contact. Housing Matters is designed for use by housing agency staff, real estate development company personnel, management company employees, and members of the public seeking and residing in affordable housing. The functionality within Housing Matters can be accessed from all modern web browsers and devices.
